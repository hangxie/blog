It’s based on when you joint the company, so no surprise – I’m the first one to pick up a cube though I don’t care the location too much. The new floor plan is just an open area, so don’t think you can get a private or hard-to-been-observed place. Seriously, I’m thinking of sitting on the ground so that walls can be still high enough to keep me hiding, but I believe people around me will feel nervous thinking of how weird their neighbour is.<\/p>\n
