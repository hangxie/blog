It used to be one of the most reliable and cute (or cool?) service on the Web, but seems not any more.<\/p>\n

I tried to move two of my sites to Google App, but had to give up because all sorts of troubles it gave to me. Yesterday I was able to move one site to it, but at the last step (to activate my E-Mail account, I guess), it kept telling my my Google account was webmaster@gmail.com and I cannot change it. I tried to move to webmaster@mydomain.com but failed due to it had been registered, and if I want to hook up with this existing account it told me that I need to use webmaster@gmail.com. I was totally lost of what I should do, so I simply removed my domain from it.<\/p>\n

It seems Google needs sometime to finish the work of removing my site as I no longer can register it, so I tried another domain I own, which is currently running on some other service providers (free, as well). Now Google is doing much better … I mentioned much better because it simply give me “Server error” (I guess it is HTTP 5xx but I’m not sure), so I don’t have to go through all the registration process and stuck in some middle way, hehe.<\/p>\n

So, good-bye to Google App, for now, I will come back and check things out later on, maybe weeks, maybe months.<\/p>\n

BTW, it could be Google is not willing to provide free service any more, so Google’s technical team should not be blamed, isn’t it?<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
