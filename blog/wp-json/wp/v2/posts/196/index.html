So I think … may be the air is the best listener, and I’m trying to talk to air now.<\/p>\n

There are always something that you do want to speak up, but if the topic is pretty bothering then you would like not to disturb others, as spread the negative feeling is not the right thing to do. For most time you know you don’t need a feedback, just someone who can listen to you and then that’s it.<\/p>\n

So the air is perfect for this – it is at anywhere I can be now, it is there for all the time, it is gentle and nice, never complains.
